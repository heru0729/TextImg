TextImg — Free Text Image Generator

TextImg is a free online tool that lets you create custom images from text instantly — no sign-up, no installation required. Choose from multiple fonts, apply effects, and download your image as PNG, JPEG, or WebP. Perfect for creating thumbnails, banners, social media posts, OGP images, and more.

✍️ Text & Fonts

Supports Japanese and English fonts including Noto Sans JP, Noto Serif JP, M PLUS Rounded, Dela Gothic One, and Zen Kaku Gothic New. Adjust font size, line height, bold, and italic freely.

🎨 Effects

Apply Pixel Mode, Outline, Drop Shadow, Glitch, Gradient Text, and Neon effects to your text. Per-character color and Gaming rainbow mode are also available.

🖼️ Export

Download your creation as PNG, JPEG, or WebP. Supports preset sizes including OGP (1200×630), square (1080×1080), FHD (1920×1080), and custom dimensions up to 4000px.

🎮 Dot Mode

Switch to Dot Mode to draw pixel art directly on the canvas. Set grid size with a slider, click to place dots, and click again to erase. Great for retro-style designs.
