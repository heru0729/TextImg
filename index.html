TextImg — Free Text Image Generator

TextImg is a free online tool that lets you create custom images from text — no sign-up or installation required. Choose from multiple fonts, apply effects, and download as PNG, JPEG, or WebP.

✍️ Text & Fonts

Supports Noto Sans JP, Noto Serif JP, M PLUS Rounded, Dela Gothic One, Zen Kaku Gothic New and more. Adjust size, line height, bold and italic freely.

🎨 Effects

Pixel Mode, Outline, Drop Shadow, Glitch, Gradient Text and Neon. Per-character color and Gaming rainbow mode also available.

🖼️ Export

Download as PNG, JPEG or WebP. Preset sizes include OGP (1200×630), Square (1080×1080), FHD (1920×1080) and custom up to 4000px.

🎮 Dot Mode

Draw pixel art directly on the canvas. Adjustable grid size, click to place dots, click again to erase.

💾 Auto Save

Settings are automatically saved to local storage and restored when you reopen the page.

📱 Mobile Friendly

Fully usable on smartphones. Canvas appears at the top with settings below for easy mobile use.
